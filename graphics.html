GRAPHICS

At UVA, I created graphics for multiple organizations including the University Programs Council, HackCville, and VSA@UVA. I always make sure each graphic has a story to tell. I highlighted some of my favorite projects below but contact me for my full portfolio :)

VSA STICKERS

Giving back always warms me up. As a board member for VSA, I wanted to come up with a creative way to raise money for Messengers of Love. Thus, I designed these stickers and sold them as a way to raise extra money for Messengers of Love while promoting VSA’s semiannual “Phofest.” My campaign proved to be successful as over 100 units were sold in under a week. It was rewarding seeing my designs make an impact on our community.

UPC EVENT QR CODE

As a marketing analyst for UPC, my director came to me with the issue of how the previous year’s “Artisan Cheeses & Masterpieces” was met with chaos as there was not an effective way to keep track of student’s pottery for pick-up. I took the initiative to make a graphic with a QR code that links to a Google form that made pick-up streamlined.

HACKCVILLE GRAPHIC

AAs a HackCville marketing coordinator, I was tasked with creative ways to advertise for the annual NYC Start-Up Trip. After research on how to use Adobe Illustrator, I crafted a flyer that was used in internal campaigns (Slack, email, etc). The use of a QR code increased traction to the website and conversions for the event.

STARBUCKS DALGONA

Dalgona whipped coffee was all the rage during the beginning of stay-at-home orders. This inspired me to make a mock-up Instagram advertisement for Starbucks with the drink. Deviating a bit from the brand image, I made this graphic more catered towards younger audiences.

F21 SPLASH PAGE

While doing research on website development, I learned that splash pages are an effective way to increase conversions on a website. This inspired me to mock up a splash page for Forever 21 with a 21% sale. I stayed true to the company’s aesthetic and brand image by incorporating bold colors and a mixture of fonts.
